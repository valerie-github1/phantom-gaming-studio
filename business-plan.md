**Phoenix AI Subsidiary | Confidential | April 2026**

## 1. Executive Summary

Phantom Gaming Studio is an AI-native game development company operating as a wholly owned subsidiary of Phoenix AI (PhoennixAI Ltd). We design and ship interactive entertainment products that use generative AI, real-time 3D rendering, and holographic UI design as first-class features — not afterthoughts.

Our first commercial product, **Phantom Drive**, is a holographic EV cockpit simulator that has completed its prototype phase. The studio is seeking pre-seed investment to bring it to market on Steam and via direct B2B licensing to EV manufacturers.

**Key metrics (target Year 1):**
- 10,000 Steam units sold @ £14.99 avg → £149,900
- 2 B2B licensing deals @ £25,000 each → £50,000
- **Total Year 1 revenue target: £199,900**

---

## 2. Problem

Gaming is the world's largest entertainment category (£180B market, 2025), yet AI integration remains superficial. Most studios bolt on "AI features" as marketing copy. No studio has built a game from the ground up where the AI is the engine — not a chatbot skin on top.

At the same time, the EV industry needs compelling digital cockpit experiences that go beyond static dashboards. Phantom Drive solves both problems simultaneously.

---

## 3. Solution

**Phantom Drive** — a real-time holographic EV telemetry experience:
- 3D WebGL cockpit with live physics simulation (speed, battery, RPM, range, temp)
- Three AI-tuned drive modes: ECO / SPORT / HYPER
- Procedural audio engine (Web Audio API)
- Built on React + Three.js + @react-three/fiber

The same core engine powers both the consumer game and the B2B white-label cockpit product.
